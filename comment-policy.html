Blog Comment Policy

First things first: WI love comments and appreciate the time that you, the reader spend to share ideas and give feedback. I'd like to genuinely thank you for that

However, I also want the comments to be as useful as possible to all of the readers. While I will keep 99% of comments, I will remove these:

  1. Harassing comments: While conversation and the sharing of different ideas is encouraged, all comments need to be respectful towards me and other commenters.
  2. Anonymous comments: I only accept comments from people who identify themselves.
  3. Promotional comments: If a comment is solely promotional in nature, I will remove it from the site.

I reserve the right to remove any comments from my site , please leave comments that are respectful and useful.

If you have any questions on the commenting policy, please let me know.
